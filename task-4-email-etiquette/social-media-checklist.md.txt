# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
### Student Digital Ambassador Guide

As a college student, your digital footprint acts as a secondary resume. Use the following guidelines to maintain a professional and safe online presence.

| Category | Do's (Best Practices) | Don'ts (Risks to Avoid) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Privacy** | Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts. | Don't post your home address, phone number, or live location. |
| **Professionalism** | Use a clean, professional headshot for LinkedIn and GitHub. | Don't post content featuring illegal substances or offensive language. |
| **Networking** | Personalize connection requests when reaching out to alumni. | Don't "spam" recruiters or professors with multiple follow-up messages. |
| **Integrity** | Cite sources and give credit when sharing others' work. | Don't participate in cyberbullying or "cancel culture" attacks. |
| **Fact-Checking** | Verify news from multiple sources before sharing. | Don't spread unverified rumors or "fake news" headlines. |

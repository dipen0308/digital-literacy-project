# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online: Student Prevention Checklist
*A guide for navigating the digital world safely and responsibly.*

---

### 💳 Financial & UPI Safety (Critical)
- [ ] **The "No PIN" Rule**: Never enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code to *receive* money. [cite_start]If a "buyer" asks for your PIN, it is 100% a scam[cite: 83].
- [ ] **Verify Every Request**: Before tapping "Pay" on apps like PhonePe or Google Pay, read the screen carefully. [cite_start]A "Payment Request" means money is *leaving* your account[cite: 83].
- [ ] **Protect Your OTP/CVV**: No bank official or customer care agent will ever ask for your OTP or CVV over a call or WhatsApp. [cite_start]Keep these private[cite: 83].

### 🔐 Account & Data Security
- [ ] [cite_start]**Enable MFA/2FA**: Turn on Multi-Factor Authentication for your college email, LinkedIn, and Instagram to prevent unauthorized logins[cite: 82].
- [ ] **Use Strong Passphrases**: Avoid easy-to-guess passwords like your name or birthdate. [cite_start]Use a unique passphrase for every platform[cite: 82].
- [ ] [cite_start]**Update Regularly**: Keep your phone's OS and apps updated to ensure you have the latest security patches against hackers[cite: 82].

### 🌐 Browsing & Social Safety
- [ ] [cite_start]**Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking**: Never log into sensitive accounts using free Wi-Fi in canteens or malls without a VPN[cite: 82].
- [ ] [cite_start]**Think Before You Click**: Verify any "urgent" links received via DM or SMS, even if they seem to come from a friend, before clicking[cite: 82].
- [ ] [cite_start]**Privacy Check**: Set your social profiles to private and avoid sharing your Aadhaar, college ID, or hostel room number publicly[cite: 82].

### 🚨 How to Report Cybercrime
[cite_start]If you are targeted by fraud or harassment, act immediately using these official channels[cite: 84]:

| Platform | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **National Portal** | File a formal complaint at [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) |
| **Emergency Helpline** | **Call 1930** (24/7) immediately to block fraudulent transactions |
| **Bank Support** | Call your bank's official toll-free number to freeze your account |

> [cite_start]**Ambassador Tip:** Reporting within the "Golden Hour" (the first 2 hours) significantly increases the chance of recovering lost funds[cite: 84].
